Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is a classic Italian dish made with layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, this recipe will give you a hearty and satisfying meal.

The combination of savory ground beef, tangy tomato sauce, and melted mozzarella makes every bite comforting and delicious. Follow these simple steps and enjoy a homemade lasagna that rivals your favorite Italian restaurant.

Ingredients

  • 9–12 lasagna noodles
  • 400 g ground beef (or mixed meat)
  • 400 g tomato sauce (or chopped tomatoes)
  • 250 g ricotta (or cottage cheese) + 200 g shredded mozzarella
  • 1 onion, 2 garlic cloves, salt, black pepper, oregano

Steps

  1. Prepare the sauce: Sauté onion and garlic, add the ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in tomato sauce and spices, then simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven: Set oven to 180°C (350°F). Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions (or use no-boil noodles).
  3. Prepare cheese mixture: Mix ricotta or cottage cheese with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. Layer the lasagna: In a baking dish, layer sauce, noodles, cheese mixture, and shredded mozzarella. Repeat to make 2–3 layers.
  5. Bake: Top with mozzarella and bake for 30–40 minutes. Let rest for 5–10 minutes before serving.
